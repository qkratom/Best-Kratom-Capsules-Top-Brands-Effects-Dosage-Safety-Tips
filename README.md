## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long do kratom capsules take to work?

Kratom capsules typically take **30-60 minutes** to produce noticeable effects, longer than powder due to capsule dissolution time. Taking capsules on an empty stomach accelerates onset, while food consumption may delay effects by 15-30 minutes. Peak effects usually occur 1-2 hours after consumption and last 4-6 hours depending on dosage and individual metabolism.

### What's the difference between gelatin and vegetarian kratom capsules?

**Gelatin capsules** dissolve faster (15-20 minutes) and cost less, but aren't suitable for vegetarians or those with religious dietary restrictions. **Vegetarian capsules** (made from plant cellulose) take slightly longer to dissolve (20-30 minutes), cost more, but accommodate all dietary preferences and may be more stable in humid conditions.

Yes, kratom capsules can be opened to access the powder for mixing with beverages or food. This method combines capsule convenience for storage with powder's faster onset time. ### How should kratom capsules be stored for maximum freshness?

Store kratom capsules in **airtight containers** away from direct sunlight, heat, and moisture. Ideal conditions include temperatures between 60-70¬∞F and humidity below 50%. Original bottles with desiccant packets work well, or transfer to glass jars with tight-fitting lids. Properly stored capsules maintain potency for 1-2 years.

### Are kratom capsules more expensive than powder?

### What's the best time of day to take kratom capsules?

Timing depends on desired effects and strain type. **White and green vein capsules** work best in morning or early afternoon for energy and focus. **Red vein capsules** are ideal for evening use promoting relaxation and sleep.
